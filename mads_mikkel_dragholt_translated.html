Mads Mikkel Dragholt

Candidate for the City Council in Copenhagen

My name is Mads Mikkel Dragholt. I am passionate about ensuring proper conditions in social and disability services. I am a former spokesperson for Autism Youth and have, among other things, worked at Reden Ung, Settlementet and WeShelter.

This has given me insight into how we can create better frameworks for the people who need help most in our city.

My political engagement stems from a strong belief that Copenhagen should be a city where everyone has the opportunity to live a good life. For me, politics is about listening, cooperating and turning words into action. If you share my vision for a more inclusive, embracing and humane city, I hope you will vote for me. Let's create a Copenhagen that is for everyone.

MY MAIN ISSUES

A TRUST-BASED AND HUMAN-CENTERED SYSTEM

– People in vulnerable situations and citizens with disabilities often encounter a mistrustful and bureaucratic system when they need help. That must change!

COMBAT CHILD PROSTITUTION

– 3.5% of students in 9th grade have sold sexual services. We have a responsibility to ensure that no child sells their body. Detection, prevention and immediate assistance are the way forward!

INCLUSION WITH THE CHILD AT THE CENTER

– Inclusion classes must be established where there are the right competencies and enough resources to include students with special needs. In addition, it should be made easier to attend a special school.

I ALSO WORK FOR:

  • More and better housing offers.
  • A strengthened effort against homelessness.
  • A dignified life for people with intellectual disabilities.
